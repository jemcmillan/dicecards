What is DICE?

Design Thinking + Communication

The DICE Model applies design thinking to the communication model to provide a framework to better understand problems and opportunities and to improve critical and creative thinking.

Structured + Creative

The DICE model provides a structured way of thinking about any communication task, issue, problem, or opportunity. It can be applied when planning a straight-forward photo shoot, when designing a command media campaign, or when developing an enterprise-wide communication plan for a wicked problem.
