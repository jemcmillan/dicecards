Mindset: Divergent Thinking

Research and Empathy

Goal

The goal here is to discover as much information as possible about the problem, the stakeholders, and the constituents, or audience. Through research and empathy, the goal is to gain a deep understanding of the issue and the people affected by it.

  1. Discovery: Learn as much as possible using qualitative and quantatative research.
  2. Open-Minded: Set aside assumptions and gain insights into users and their needs through empathy. Use empathy maps, journey maps, and personas.
  3. Expansive: Move up the ladder of abstraction.
  4. Breadth: Consider associated problems, issues or opportunities.

Tools

  • Qualatative Research
  • Quantatative Research
  • Empathy Maps
  • Journey Maps
  • Personas
  • Ladder of Abstraction
