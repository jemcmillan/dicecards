Deliver the Plan

The goal during this part of the IDEATE stage is to select viable solutions that are likely to solve the problem and to develop the plan that will be implemented in the CREATE stage.

  1. Select viable solutions
  2. Develop plan

Select Solutions

  1. Add constraints. Up to this point in the IDEATION phase, you've been working in a world where there were no constraints. That's great for coming up with creative solutions and new ideas, but it's not realistic when it comes to execution. There are constraints in everything we do.
    • Time
    • Money
    • People
    • Technology
    • Support
  2. Viability. Is it viable? Before selecting a course of action and building a plan on how to achieve it, you have to honestly destermine its chance for success.
