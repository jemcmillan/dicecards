Mindset: Divergent Thinking

Collect & Coordinate

Collection Goals

Ensure your content collection includes:

  1. Multiple sources. Get more interviews. Get more information. Get more data. Two interview sources is a minimum for most stories, but you should strive for three or more.
  2. Multiple media. Do you have enough for stories in multiple media? Audio? Video? Still Imagery? If you're doing a print-based story, get audio and video. If you're doing a video-based story, get still imagery. If you're doing a photo-based story, get sound. The idea is to collect for transmedia and multimedia stoytelling.
  3. Enough for content layering. The goal here is to collect more content than you plan on using. Do you have enough information to layer the information? Did you collect enough to create a transmedia story?

Coordination Goals

Communication doesn't happen alone. It will take coordination to collect usable content and to create meaningful experiences. The goal here is to consider everyone who needs to be contacted for the project and/or story.

Ask yourself the following:

  1. Who needs to know? Stateholders? Constituents? Colloborators?
  2. Who needs to approve portions of the plan?
  3. Who should be made aware of the plan? Up, down, across the organization.

Time spent coordinatinating upfront will typically end up being valuable for the project either by adding new perspectives, gaining better access, or providing new constraints that force creativity.
